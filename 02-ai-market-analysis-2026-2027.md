## Technology Adoption Waves

### Wave 1 (2023–2024): Experimentation
- ChatGPT wrappers, basic RAG demos
- Shadow IT pilots in enterprises

### Wave 2 (2025–2026): Productization
- Copilots embedded in SaaS (CRM, support, dev tools)
- RAG on internal knowledge bases
- Evaluation and guardrails become mandatory

### Wave 3 (2026–2027): Agentic Scale ← **Curriculum focus**
- Multi-agent workflows replacing brittle automation
- AI employees / browser agents / code agents
- LLMOps maturity: CI/CD for prompts and models
- Compliance and AI TRiSM (Trust, Risk, Security Management)

## What Employers Reject in 2026–2027

- Resume with only Jupyter notebooks, no deployed URLs
- “Built a ChatGPT clone” with no eval metrics or architecture doc
- No understanding of cost, latency, or failure modes
- Inability to explain RAG chunking or agent state management in interviews
- Missing security awareness (prompt injection, data leakage)

## Strategic Implication for Students

The US market rewards **engineers who ship measurable production AI**, not researchers who only know theory. This curriculum optimizes for Wave 3 skills: agents, LLMOps, enterprise governance, and cloud-scale deployment.
