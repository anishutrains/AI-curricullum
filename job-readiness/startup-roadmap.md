# AI SaaS Startup Roadmap

## When This Path Fits

- You want ownership over product and equity
- You have domain insight (healthcare, legal, sales, etc.)
- You can tolerate 12–24 months runway risk

## 0→1 Sequence (16 Weeks)

| Week | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 1–2 | Problem interviews (10 target users) |
| 3–4 | Thin vertical slice — one workflow, one persona |
| 5–8 | MVP from Module 09 SaaS patterns |
| 9–10 | 5 design partners on free pilot |
| 11–12 | Measure activation + retention proxy |
| 13–14 | Pricing test ($49–$299/mo SMB) |
| 15–16 | Decide: pivot, persevere, or raise pre-seed |

## Technical Stack (Default)

- **Frontend:** Next.js + Vercel
- **Backend:** FastAPI on Railway/Fly
- **AI:** OpenAI/Anthropic + RAG (Pinecone/pgvector)
- **Auth:** Clerk
- **Billing:** Stripe usage-based
- **Analytics:** PostHog

## YC-Style Criteria

| Question | Good Answer |
|----------|-------------|
| Who desperately needs this? | Specific role + workflow |
| Why now? | AI makes X 10× cheaper |
| Why you? | Domain + shipped demo |
| Moat? | Data flywheel, workflow lock-in, compliance |

## Fundraising (Optional)

- Pre-seed: $500K–$2M on prototype + design partners
- Investors want: growth rate, retention, technical founder who ships

## Course Project → Startup

Best course projects to productize:

1. AI Workflow Automation Platform
2. AI Sales Assistant (vertical CRM)
3. Enterprise RAG (vertical knowledge base)
