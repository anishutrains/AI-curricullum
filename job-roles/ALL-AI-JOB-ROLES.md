# Phase 2 — All Major AI Job Roles (USA 2026–2027)

Each role includes: description, salary range, demand, growth, skills, portfolio projects, enterprise expectations.

**Salary notes:** Ranges are US base salary 2026; total comp often +30–50% with equity at startups/FAANG.
